# Transportation

## Venue

[National Taiwan University of Science and Technology]

:leaflet-map

## Public Transportation

### :icon{name="local:trtc"} MRT

1. Take :icon{name="local:green-line"} Green Line to G07 Gongguan Station
2. Exit from Exit 2 "Mingchuan Elementary School"
3. Turn left onto Zhoushan Road
4. Turn right at Lu Ming Hall
5. Cross Keelung Road and turn left
6. Arrive at National Taiwan University of Science and Technology

### :icon{name="local:bus"} Bus

Take the following bus routes to the venue. Click on the route name to view detailed real-time bus information, stop locations, and route maps.

:bus-routes

### :icon{name="tabler:plane"} From Taoyuan Airport to Taipei Main Station

Take the :icon{name="local:airport-mrt"} Taoyuan Airport MRT Express to Taipei Main Station, approximately 40 minutes. After arriving at Taipei Main Station, transfer to Taipei MRT :icon{name="local:red-line"} Tamsui-Xinyi Line, transfer at Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall Station to :icon{name="local:green-line"} Songshan-Xindian Line to Gongguan.

## Driving and Parking

### Routes

#### Via Sun Yat-sen Freeway (National Highway No. 1)

Sun Yat-sen Freeway → Exit at Yuanshan Interchange → Take Jianguo Expressway → Exit at Xinhai Road towards Muzha → Turn right at the intersection of Xinhai Road Sec. 2 and Keelung Road → Pass Changxing Street → National Taiwan University of Science and Technology

#### Via Formosa Freeway (National Highway No. 3)

Formosa Freeway → Exit at Taipei Connecting Road → Turn left at the intersection of Xinhai Road Sec. 3 and Keelung Road → Changxing Street → National Taiwan University of Science and Technology
